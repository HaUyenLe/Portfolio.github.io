About

Bio
Ha Uyen Le is an artist born in Bac Lieu, Vietnam and raised in San Jose, California. Before moving to the U.S in 2nd grade, she lived in Vietnam where she often visits her relatives in the countryside where she grows to love nature and it open and vast sceneries. This love of nature influences her art works. She discovered her love for art in kindergarten when she participated in her school art completion where she created her first ever artwork of a landscape out of playdoh. As someone who is shy and introverted, art is her source of communication and a way to release her stress. Her medium of choice is pencil and color pencil because it was always available for her since was young and is the medium that she is most comfortable with.
